Question: Can you use either TetherAll tag to find its mate?

A: Yes, TetherAll works both ways. You can find item A from item B or vice versa.

Question: Can you attach two TetherAll tags to more than one item?

A: Yes. The TetherAll Tag system needs at least two to work, but you can combine as many as you want.

Question: Does the TetherAll system rely on my phone to find objects?

A: Only when it's your phone you're looking for! In that case, a TetherAll tag has to be attached to your phone. But your phone doesn't have to be powered on to be found and you don't have to have your phone to find other tagged items.

Question: Can you set your own preferences for the alert sounds?

A: Yes, you can have to a steady sound (volume levels differ) or gradual sound.

Question: What could you attach to the TetherAll?

A: The possibilities are endless! You could have one on your phone, on your keys, wallet and so on. You could even throw one in a bag while you are in your car to remember to take that in as you get home. TetherAll will help you keep track of any item, device, gun, anything.

Question: Will the alert go off if I get too far from the item in my own home?

A: No. you can set your home as your "Home Base," that way the device won't drive you crazy every time you walk into the other room. However, if you are trying to locate a TetherAll-tagged item in your home, there is always the button option to sound off the alarm to find that object.
