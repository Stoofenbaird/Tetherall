TetherAll is a unique device locator

It allows consumers to locate their personal devices even when the battery power is exhausted.

TetherAll is a 2 part tagging device.

One tag can be attached to any item, i.e. cellphone, keys, computer, wallet..whatever you need to keep track of. The other can be placed in your pocket, key ring, purse, etc. When one tag moves outside a predetermined boundary, which is set by the user, an alert will sound, notifying you that you have left your item behind.
